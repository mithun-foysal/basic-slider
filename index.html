This is Content Titel

Elusive Targets are specialized contracts, and will only surface once. These contracts made by Square Enix, have new stories, looks, and ways to kill and die. Once you die you can no longer play the Elusive Contract. You can however, restart the Elusive Contract at any point. However, exceptions may be added. For example, with Howard Moxon, you could not restart the contract after you've either acquired the Ivory White or killed Moxon himself. You may not restart an Elusive Target after you've killed the target, or begun combat or are hunted by hostiles.
